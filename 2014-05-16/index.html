Notes

The building is implemented as a replication of the apartment through 4 floors. A landing enhances communication between side A (the original apartment) and side B (a mirrored apartment with respect to side A).
Some primitives (such as tree, stair ecc..) are taken and reused from former homeworks (expecially the second one).
The project is meant to be highly modular and parametric.
